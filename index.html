The key challenge to the safety of children online is that most children discover the internet not through teachers or parental guidance but via peers.

They get exposed to contents they should not be exposed to, they get mis-socialized and maladjusted to carry out anti-social behaviour that are contrary to the culture and social norms of their society.

CYBER

Teaching children about safety online is one thing, taking precaution to ensure that children are safe online is different. This means that school must also have in place children protection online policies and enforcement mechanisms. Most schools do not have guidelines on working with children whether offline or

HYGIENCE

online, so we end up having our children entrusted in the hands of child predators

All schools must take appropriate measures to address risk factors and prevent Abuse and Exploitation before it occurs.

Prevention measures should include

ONLINE SAFETY

Procedures and codes of conduct for protecting Youth;

Capacity building for all those who work with Youth;

Awareness raising and good practice for minimizing the risks to Youth.
